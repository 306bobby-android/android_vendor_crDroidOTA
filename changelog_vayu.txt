**** PLEASE READ UPDATE INSTRUCTIONS ****

* Clean flash is required (You can try dirty flash via OTA, if bootloop, reboot to twrp and format data)
* After OTA download is done, you can long hold on downloaded update and choose export button to save where you want. (default path: root/data/crdroid_updates)
* Reboot to recovery.
* Flash update. (No Gapps reflash needed)
* Wipe Cache/Dalvik.
* Format data (type: yes)
* Reboot to system.
