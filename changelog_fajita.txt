Well, guess I missed a month. Hopefully that won't happen too often. Let's see, highlights since... v7.6:

*** Common:
- Vendor blobs from OOS 10.3.11.
- July ASB merged (so no going back to earlier without formatting /data partition).
- Included OnePlusCamera v2.10.8 (oneplus signed, can be updated with newer official APKs, but they don't work so good yet).
- Switched Google Camera port to DMGC v4.5 (based on GCam v7.3.020), has working slow-motion at 1/4 and 1/8 realtime out of the box.
- Huge device tree rewrite & rebase for future ease of maintaining (based on lineage-18.1 then removed Android 11-specific changes from cr6 branches).
- Shipping with mcd kernel (arter97 base) r5 modded to build inline with our device tree particulars. Will keep updated as mcd sources get updated (join the TG group for info on how to help test).
- If you've been on an older official release for a while, please note that there's a bunch of device-specific customization in Settings > OnePlus Settings. If you formatted data during installation, it might not show up until you've restarted once after finishing setup wizard.
- Clean install recommended due to all the changes over the last few months (see channel notes).

*** cr7-specific:
- Passes safetynet out of the box (assuming you have GApps installed), yay. This might not be new, but I finally tested it. Recommend NikGapps core + addons or openga
pps pico/nano (Flame gapps basic works as well but it clobbers the crDroid setup wizard, MindTheGapps should also work but I haven't tried it).
- No built-in TWRP yet, stay tuned.

*** Fajita-specific:
- Disabled "screen off FOD" because it wasn't working right, and trying to keep it was breaking other things. FOD works better now, and you can always enable the Ambient Display "pulse notifications on pocket removal/pickup/tilt" so the screen is awake when you grab the phone to scan your thumb; works great unlocking with a single touch that way.
- Removed "Disable night light for FOD" function, because it's actually broken right now. If you use night light, recommend re-capturing fingerprints with FOD circle color set to cyan (so that it skews toward green with the night light tint applied).
