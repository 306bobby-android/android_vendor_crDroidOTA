It's been a minute, hey?
Highlights since... woof, 6.13? 6.16? Let's see...

*** Common:
- Vendor blobs from OOS 10.3.11.
- July ASB merged (so no going back to earlier without formatting /data partition).
- Included OnePlusCamera v2.10.8 (oneplus signed, can be updated with newer official APKs, but they don't work so good yet).
- Switched Google Camera port to DMGC v4.5 (based on GCam v7.3.020), has working slow-motion at 1/4 and 1/8 realtime out of the box.
- Huge device tree rewrite & rebase for future ease of maintaining (based on lineage-18.1 then removed Android 11-specific changes from cr6 branches).
- Shipping with mcd kernel (arter97 base) r5 modded to build inline with our device tree particulars. Will keep updated as mcd sources get updated (join the TG group for info on how to help test).
- If you've been on an older official release for a while, please note that there's a bunch of device-specific customization in Settings > OnePlus Settings. If you formatted data during installation, it might
not show up until you've restarted once after finishing setup wizard.
- Clean install recommended due to all the changes over the last few months (see channel notes).

*** cr6-specific:
- Aux camera doesn't work correctly due to framework issues beyond my control/comprehension. So, still no portrait mode in OnePlusCamera in cr6. 4Kp60 video recording works great; huzzah!
- Doesn't pass safetynet out of the box, despite many tests with different fingerprints/props. Just use Magisk & enable MagiskHide and be happy. If your bank's app doesn't work, switch to a local credit union
and stop feeding the bougie bankers.
- For some reason, the FOD icon seems to have an alpha layer applied to it based on screen brightness... since cr6 frameworks are frozen, most likely won't get fixed. Sorry.
- Built-in TWRP updated to latest 3.5.2_9-0.
